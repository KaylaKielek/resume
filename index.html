Ms. Kielek's Learning Profile

"Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up" -Pablo Picasso

I am a teacher...

because I know the importance that this profession holds and I want to help children reach their potential. I want to make a difference in students’ lives and I couldn’t think of a better way to accomplish that goal than to teach. I have a strong passion for children as well as making a difference in the world and because of this, I am very passionate about my teaching practices.

I believe that...

it critical to focus on the development of the whole child while teaching and not just the intellectual side of a child. In order to achieve this goal, I will focus on the integration of arts education in the classroom. Integrating various forms of the arts such as drama, music, and fine arts, helps to create robust lessons that encourage emotional, intellectual, social, and physical development in students. I also understand the need for students to be engaged in the world around them and believe that it is crucial to connect curriculum to a student’s life as well as connect curriculum to world issues. I will strive to achieve these goals by making curriculum relevant while also facilitating the whole-child development of my students.

I also hope...

to inspire my students to dream big and I will aim to give them the foundation they need to achieve their dreams. I will do this by contributing a diverse range of opportunities for my students in order to discover more about themselves and their learning by providing my students with various engaging activities on a day-to-day basis.

Most importantly...

I will strive to make meaningful relationships with my students. I will take interested in my student’s lives, learn about their hobbies and interests, and be there for them as a support. I will laugh and have fun with my students during class time and show them how fun learning can truly be. By building these meaningful relationships with students, I will help create a welcoming and safe environment in the classroom that is built on mutual trust and respect.
